About Us

JeRS AC Gym Equipment is a company specializing in the sales of gym and exercise equipment. The company was founded in August 2014 and is located in the Gyy Building, Kristong Hari, Tomas Morato, Quezon City, Philippines. At JeRS AC Gym Equipment, our mission is to provide top-quality gym and exercise equipment to help our clients achieve their fitness goals. Our knowledgeable and friendly staff is always available to assist with product selection and answer any questions. We offer a wide range of gym equipment, from free weights and weightlifting machines to cardio equipment and accessories. At JeRS AC Gym Equipment, we believe in providing a complete fitness experience for our clients. That's why we offer a range of services, including equipment installation, maintenance, and repair. Cardio Machines

Post by : Den 20-12-2019

are exercise equipment designed to improve cardiovascular health and fitness by providing a low-impact, aerobic workout. They typically work by simulating exercises such as running, cycling, or rowing, but with less impact on the joints. Cardio machines are commonly found in gyms and fitness centers, and can be used for a variety of workout routines.
